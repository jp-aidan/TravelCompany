About Us

Hi, JP Cavalcante and Aidan Carrigy here, thank you for taking the time to visit our website and we hope you find our site easy to follow and engaging.

We were fellow college students that decided to set up C & C Tours in 2021 to cater for the escorted small group side of the tourism industry here in Ireland. The name C & C Tours comes from both our last names. As consumer confidence returns post pandemic, we spotted a niche in the market as people return to travel but may want to do so privately in smaller groups and not on a coach tour with 40+ people. That is why our company only covers a max of 5 passengers in one vehicle.

We have a fleet of modern vehicles and a team of driver-guides who are the best in the business that offer excellent service and make your holiday a unique experience. Some of our drivers have over two decades of experience in the industry so there is no corner of Ireland they cannot take you.

Thank you for time and we look forward to touring with you soon.

Meet Aidan and JP

Aidan

Aidan is a native of Ireland and qualified as a National Tour Guide with Distinction through Failte Ireland, which is the Irish governing tourism body, in 2013. He has over a decade of experience as a Tour Director leading tours around Scotland & Ireland for some of Ireland’s largest tour operators. He is an active driver-guide for the company while also covering the digital marketing side of the business.

JP

Coming from a background in education and hospitality he has excellent communication skills and deals with the customer service side of the business.
